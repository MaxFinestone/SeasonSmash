Season Smash

Pixel Duels

Play Season Smash with a buddy anytime, immersing yourselves in the magical and serene world of snowball fights and fire. Choose to battle amidst the fiery heart of a volcano, the cheerful snowy mountains, or journey to the future in Neo Tokyo, where futuristic plasma reigns supreme, invented in the year 3036. Assume the roles of Antarcticans Leo and Darwin as they fight for the answer to their research, striving to win the Nobel Prize in 2024. However, only one can emerge victorious. Leo or Darwin: who will it be?

Note: the project aimed to facilitate friendly competition among friends and family, offering breaks from work to replenish neurotransmitters, boost dopamine levels, and improve concentration for more effective work sessions. Spend just five minutes playing with a buddy to enhance productivity.

Rating: 9.5

Developer: Maxence Finestone

Released: December 2023

Technology: HTML, CSS, JS, Unity (Unity WebGL)
